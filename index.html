Americano

Ingredients

  • 2 Shots of Espresso
  • 11/4 Cups of Hot Water

How to make it

  1. Boil the water and pour it into your favorite mug.
  2. Slowly pour the brewed espresso into the hot water.

Cappuccino

Ingredients

  • 1 Shot of Espresso
  • 2/3 of Cup Milk of Your Choice
  • A Pinch of Cinnamon or Chocolate Shavings (optional)

How to make it

  1. brew one shot of espresso
  2. froth your warmed milk or dairy alternative
  3. pour the espresso shot into your favorite mug
  4. add the frothed milk or dairy alternative
  5. sprink the cinnamon or chocolate shavings on top
